Methodology & Sources

This assessment draws on publicly available institutional research to estimate the probability of AI-driven disruption for a given role. The scoring model incorporates:

  • Role category classification — job titles mapped to disruption risk tiers based on task automation probability studies
  • Experience modifiers — tenure affects resilience (junior roles more exposed to automation; senior roles retain strategic value)
  • AI tool adoption — current usage of AI tools is a leading indicator of adaptability
  • Seniority adjustments — executive and senior roles are buffered by decision-making authority and organizational relationships
